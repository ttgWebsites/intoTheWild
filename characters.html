Characters

  • John Krakauer
  • The author and narrator of Into the Wild
  • He has a similar life story to Chris McCandless. He was obsessed with mountain climbing, and even took a dangerous adventure to Alaska to climb the Devil's Thumb
  • Had a problem with authority figures, particularly male, due to the relationship he had with his father
  • Christopher McCandless
  • The protagonist of the novel, Chris' story of his travel across North America and ultimate death in Alaskan Yukon territory
  • Chris was a very passionate and intense young man, he had strong ideals which he followed very intensely
  • Did not have a very good relationship with his parents, particularly with his father, who he learned cheated on his mother
  • Attended Emory University and graduated before he began his journey
  • Died in the summer of 1992 on his expedition into the wilderness of Alaska
  • Also went by "Alex Supertramp"
  • Jim Gallien
  • The last person to speak to Chris before his death
  • He picked up Chris just outside of Fairbanks, and drove him to the trail where Chris would begin his wilderness journey
  • Ken Thompson, Gordon Samel, Ferdie Swanson
  • These three men are hunting for moose when they discover Chris' body
  • Wayne Westerberg
  • Owner of a grain elevator in South Dakota
  • He employs Chris and the two become friends, Chris is in contact with him through mail up until he sets out into the wilderness
  • Walt McCandless
  • Chris' father, the two have a difficult relationship, as Walt has high expectations of Chris and is rather hard on him
  • He is rather wealthy and is an aerospace engineer
  • Cheats on Chris' mother Billie early on in their relationship, Chris learns of this which causes a greater schism between the two
  • Billie McCandless
  • Chris' mother, she is a very passionate women, and known to have quite a temper
  • Her relationship is better with Chris than Walt's relationship with Chris, however the two are very distant at the time of Chris' disappearance
  • Carine McCandless
  • Chris' sister, the two are very close growing up, they are best friends
  • She understands him better than everyone, and is very similar to him in her passion, however she is also very different
  • She is very forgiving of people, something that Chris lacks, and she is also willing to conform to the normal American lifestyle
  • Jan Burres
  • A rubber tramp who meets Chris while traveling around the north-west
  • She meets Chris while he is hitchhiking and the two become close friends
  • He works with her at her flea market
  • The two keep in touch on his travels all the way up until his trip into the wilderness
  • Peter Kalitka
  • Private investigator hired to track down Chris by his parents
  • Ronald Franz
  • An elderly man who meets Chris while he is hitchhiking
  • The two develop a very close relationship with Franz even offering to adopt Chris
  • When he learns of Chris' death he renounces his fate and takes up alcohol again
  • Gaylord Stukey
  • Meets Chris on the Alaska Highway, the two meet at the Laird River Hot Springs
  • Stukey agrees to give Chris a ride to Alaska, and the two have long conversations
