Interesting Details

  • The book was turned into a motion picture in 2007 of the same name, the film was nominated for two academy awards.
  • Had McCandless traveled farther down the river he was staying near before his death, he would have found a cable crossing the river that would have allowed safe passage across, instead he was stuck on the other side, ultimately to die.
  • McCandless' cause of death as reported in this book has been argued since the book was published, several scientists have tested the plant that supposedly poisoned McCandless and claimed that this could not have killed him.
