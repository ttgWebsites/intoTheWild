Book Summary

  • So basically...
  • Into the Wild tells the true story of Chris McCandless, a young man from Maryland who forgoes the life of a normal recent college graduate and sets out on a journey across North America. McCandless journey seems to stem from a variety of reasons, his foul relationship with his parents, and his desire to release himself from living a material world, are just a few. McCandless journey concludes with a trip to the Alaskan wilderness, which he had been planning for quite a while. While in the wilderness in Alaska McCandless manages to get trapped on the wrong side of a river preventing him from returning to civilization. He eventually dies and is discovered a few weeks later his story unknown. Krakauer has managed to piece together his story and create Into the Wild.
